Cameron Tolentino

Graphic / Web Designer


Cameron Tolentino is a Digital Media graduate student attending the University of Central Florida. Before that, he obtained a B.A. in Web Development (UCF as well), and now enjoys using the knowledge from the program to create applications and installations in web development and art. At the moment, he is a research assistant working on the development of an audio-based web application.


His main research interests include narrative identity in digital culture and the effects of hyperreality on reasoning and embodiment. He is also highly interested in building models, modding computer games, and redesigning systems for modern use.


For fun, he can often be found drawing, creating interactive fiction, or chasing dogs.
