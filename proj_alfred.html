Alfred

A Non-invasive Personal Assistant

A personal assistant application creation via python. The application assists with a particular set of tasks, such as recording in a virtual bullet journal. As of today, the assistant, with Alfred as its default yet customizable name, can assist with several tasks including but not limited to:

  • Habit Tracking
  • Chore Tracking
  • Quick Research

A future update may include a graphical interface for desktop and add future functionality.
