Gumballs

The Age Old Persistence of Gacha Games

A love note to the nostalgic machines and a reflection of the childhood experiences that surround them. It was also my very first attempt in creating a comic according to a particular format.

In some ways, it's happy; in others, a little sad. It's a short story about growing older and how the way we see the world changes.
