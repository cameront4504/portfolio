The Lion & The Mouse

Revisiting one of Aesop's Tales

"You laughed when I said I would repay you," said the Mouse. "Now you see that even a Mouse can help a Lion."

A visual overhaul for one of Aesop's famous fables: The Lion & the Mouse. The constraints included sticking to the original story as well as making it fit the format of the average children's book, namely the popular 16-page format.
