[PYG]MALION*

Solve Crime in the 4th Dimension

[PYG]MALION* is an interactive story (26k+ words) where you are put in the role of The Murdered God, a former divine... now stuck in a marble statue.

Alongside a former Necromancer, you enter the House of Seven Pools in an attempt to solve the mystery of what should have been an impossible murder.

Note: Submitted to The 2021 Spring Thing Festival of Interactive Fiction.
