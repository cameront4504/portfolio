The Unrequited House of Seven Pools

A twist on the classic Who Done It?

A short deductive reasoning game built using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. It served as the original inspiration for a later project, [PYG]MALION*.

In it, you take the role of The Detective, an impartial truth seeker now set upon the latest murder mystery. Question your suspects, investigate the crime scene-- will it be enough to convict One of the Five?
